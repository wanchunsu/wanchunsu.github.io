AEyeAlliance

A Braille-to-text Converter using Computer Vision

AEyeAlliance started out as a hackathon project inspired by one of our team memberâ€™s personal experience living with a blind host mother. We continued to develop this project after winning second place at the AI for Social Good Hackathon 2018. Our AI system is now able to convert images of Braille characters into English text by employing a convolutional neural network model and a novel character segmentation method. We also created our own dataset, Braille37, which consists of multiple copies of 37 Braille character classifiers.
